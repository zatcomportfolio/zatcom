Ootemin — Art Installation & Exhibit

Artist: Ootemin (Linda Fay Lawrence) Role: Creative Collaboration, Sales & Marketing Support Focus: Northern Indigenous Art, Eagle Imagery, Cultural Storytelling Overview Ootemin is the professional artist name of Linda Fay Lawrence, a Northern Indigenous artist whose work centers on the strength, spirit, and symbolism of eagles. This project involved supporting and collaborating on a public art installation that showcased her work within a community-facing space. The collaboration focused on maintaining cultural integrity while helping position her work for broader engagement and long-term sustainability. My Role I collaborated closely with Ootemin to support both the creative presentation and the business side of her art practice. My contributions included: Supporting sales and marketing efforts for the exhibit Providing thoughtful critique to help clarify and define her artistic style Assisting in shaping a sustainable business model for her work Helping translate artistic intent into accessible storytelling for audiences This was a collaborative, relationship-based process rooted in trust, respect, and shared goals.

Process & Approach Cultural Listening: Understanding the meaning and teachings behind the eagle imagery Style Definition: Supporting consistency in scale, themes, and presentation Audience Engagement: Helping frame the work in a way that invited conversation and connection Business Clarity: Assisting with pricing, positioning, and long-term direction Outcome & Impact Delivered a successful public art installation in a community gallery/café setting Increased visibility and engagement with Ootemin’s work Strengthened clarity and confidence in her artistic direction Established groundwork for future exhibitions and collections Reflection This collaboration reinforced that design thinking extends beyond digital products. Listening, iteration, and respect for lived and cultural knowledge are essential when working with creative partners—especially in culturally grounded work.
