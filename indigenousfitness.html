Indigenous Fitness — Brand & Platform Development

Role: Founder, Brand Strategy, Digital Development

Focus: Indigenous wellness, identity-driven branding, digital presence

Overview IndigenousFitness.ca is a wellness-focused brand I have been building to promote strength, discipline, and cultural pride through fitness. The goal is to create a space where physical wellness connects with identity, community, and resilience. This project combines branding, digital development, and long-term strategic thinking.

My Role: Developed the brand concept and positioning Defined target audience and messaging direction Designed and structured the social media Built digital assets and visual identity Ongoing strategy for growth and sustainability.

Process: Clarified mission and values Defined tone and cultural alignment Built foundational social media structure Iterated on messaging for clarity and authenticity

Impact: Established a branded digital presence Created a foundation for future programming and content Positioned wellness within a culturally grounded framework.

Reflection: Building IndigenousFitness.ca has strengthened my ability to merge identity, business strategy, and digital execution. It represents long-term thinking rather than a one-off project.
